About

A small bay, a wide range of cuts.

EditKaro is a video editing and social media marketing agency based in India, working with brands and creators who need their footage turned into content people actually finish watching.

Mission

To give every client — from a solo creator to a growing brand — editing that's fast, consistent, and built around how their specific audience watches and scrolls.

Vision

To be the edit bay brands return to for every category of video they make, from a 15-second hook to a full documentary cut.

CH. 01

How a project moves through the bay

01 — Ingest

Footage & brief in

Raw footage, brand notes, and reference cuts land in a shared project folder.

02 — Assemble

Rough cut

Structure, pacing, and selects come together into a first watchable version.

03 — Grade & Mix

Color & sound

Color grading, music, sound design, and titles are layered onto the locked cut.

04 — Deliver

Export & hand-off

Platform-ready exports go out, sized and formatted for where they'll actually run.
